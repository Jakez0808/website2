Welcome To The Official Strech-n-Grow North Shore Website!

Stretch-n-Grow FUNtastic Fitness is Imaginative, Engaging and FUN! Our program follows a nationally recognized and age-appropriate scope and sequence to insure educational and physical milestones are met. This weekly early childhood physical education, nutrition and wellness program improves the level of fitness in our Stars and teaches them the importance of a healthy lifestyle and the perils of inactivity. We assist parents and childcare providers in developing good health and fitness habits! Highlights of FUNtastic Fitness: Continuous FUN with skill-specific equipment and safe and effective exercises designed for young children FUNdamental Motor Development practice Action-packed, non-stop fitness class where every child is the focus – no waiting in line! Exercise emphasizing large muscle groups, motor skills, coordination, balance, resistance, flexibility and aerobic activity Themed curriculum that reinforces concepts that are the hallmark of early childhood education Age-appropriate discussions on important topics such as exercise, health, nutrition, safety and hygiene plus monthly newsletters for parents Classes available for ages 18 months-5 years WHAT TO WEAR: Dress for action! Closed toe athletic shoes are a must!

Ilyce Zoloto is the owner and Coach for Strech-n-Grow in the North Shore, and is trusted by many schools. She spends her days teaching children about Physical Education and their health in a fun way.
